Shop with Friends
Have you ever forgotten to pick something up from the store and regretted it, ever been in a situation that you need something urgently but can't go to the store to buy it?

Well, then this app is a one-stop solution to all your problems. The app lets you create and maintain shopping lists, share them with friends. The app will also notify the user if a friend is at a store upon which he/she might add item(s) to a shopping list, he/she might want the friend to pickup.

The application will also include features like: set maximum price & quantity, request image of the product before purchase(plans to implement a barcode scanner), request for receipt, know exactly how much you owe your friend and much more.
